# 📊 E-commerce Customer Returns & Behavior Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, product returns, customer purchasing behavior, and delivery performance for an e-commerce business. The objective is to identify the key factors contributing to high product returns and provide data-driven recommendations to improve customer satisfaction, operational efficiency, and overall profitability.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

An e-commerce company is experiencing a high product return rate, resulting in revenue loss and increased operational costs. Management wants to identify the key factors driving product returns, analyze customer purchasing behavior, evaluate delivery performance, and provide data-driven recommendations to reduce returns, improve customer satisfaction, and increase overall profitability.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Measure product return rates and identify high-return categories.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior across different customer segments.
- Evaluate delivery performance and its impact on customer experience.
- Identify high-return locations.
- Provide actionable business recommendations to improve profitability.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

- **Python (Pandas, NumPy):** Data cleaning, preprocessing, handling missing values, removing duplicates, and data manipulation.
- **Power BI:** Interactive dashboard development and business intelligence reporting.
- **Power Query:** Data transformation, cleaning, and preparation.
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Created calculated measures, KPIs, and business metrics.
- **Data Modeling:** Built relationships between tables to enable efficient reporting and analysis.

# 📊 Key Business Insights

- **Total Customers:** 416
- **Total Orders:** 950
- **Average Order Value:** ₹5.27K
- **Product Return Rate:** 36.32%
- **Returned Orders:** 345
- **Highest Return Categories:** Toys (40%) and Books (40%)
- **Highest Return Locations:** Pune and Bangalore
- **Highest Revenue Location:** Chennai
- **Average Delivery Time:** 4.92 Days
- **On-Time Delivery:** 57.16%
- **Late Delivery:** 42.84%

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

| Business Area | Recommendation |
|---------------|----------------|
| Product Returns | Improve product quality checks, enhance product descriptions, and collect customer feedback to reduce returns. |
| Product Categories | Focus on Toys and Books by reviewing suppliers, improving packaging, and strengthening quality inspections. |
| Customer Retention | Introduce loyalty programs, personalized offers, and targeted marketing campaigns for repeat customers. |
| Regional Performance | Investigate the high return rates in Pune and Bangalore while replicating Chennai's successful sales strategy. |
| Delivery Performance | Optimize logistics, improve warehouse operations, and monitor courier performance to increase on-time deliveries. |
| Business Monitoring | Continuously monitor KPIs and build automated dashboards for data-driven decision making. |

# 📌 Conclusion

The dashboard identifies a high product return rate (36.32%) as the company's primary business challenge. The analysis reveals that Toys and Books have the highest return rates, Pune and Bangalore require focused improvement, and Regular customers contribute the highest revenue. While delivery performance can be optimized, the findings suggest that product quality and customer expectations are the primary drivers of returns. Implementing the recommended actions can reduce product returns, improve customer satisfaction, lower operational costs, and increase overall business profitability.
